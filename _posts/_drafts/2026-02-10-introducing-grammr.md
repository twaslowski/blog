# My most elaborate pet project

In 2023, just around the time GPT-4 was released, I was still very bright-eyed and naive about LLMs. I'd constantly
explore what could be done with them. At the time I was learning Russian, and I was hoping to build something that could
support me in my language-learning endeavors.

Three years later, I _might just_ have something.

Consider the following phrase: "Давай встретимся в среду". This means "Let's meet on Wednesday" in English.
As somebody not very familiar with Russian, I might want to know what the word "среду" means.
The answer to that is that it comes from "среда", which can mean either "Wednesday" or "environment" depending on
context. But how does a translator know which one is the right one? The answer is: by looking at the context.

In this case, the word "встретимся" (let's meet) gives us a strong hint that "среду" means "Wednesday" here.
